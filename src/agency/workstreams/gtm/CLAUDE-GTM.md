# CLAUDE-GTM.md — gtm workstream

The **gtm** workstream is go-to-market for TheAgency: growth, launch, adopter acquisition, community, partnerships, messaging, and the narrative the framework tells the outside world.

## Purpose

Turn TheAgency from an internal framework into something other teams adopt, talk about, and contribute to. This is the workstream that designs and executes launch motions, onboarding flows, and community programs.

## Scope

- **In scope:** vouch model, CONTRIBUTING / AI-POLICY documentation, starter-repo follower redirects, X/Twitter monitoring and response (e.g., `@AgencyGroupAI`), open-source launch requirements, developer account setup, adoption case studies, framework-level messaging.
- **Out of scope:** internal tooling (`housekeeping`), product-specific launches (handled by the product workstream itself), internal methodology development (`agency`).

## Conventions

- **Seeds for gtm** live at `agency/workstreams/gtm/seeds/` — launch ideas, messaging drafts, community observations.
- **Business opportunities** flagged for gtm follow the format: origin (who brought it up), opportunity shape, effort required, go/defer decision.
- **External artifacts** (tweets, blog drafts, talks) captured with timestamps and author attribution so the git history shows the editorial pipeline.
