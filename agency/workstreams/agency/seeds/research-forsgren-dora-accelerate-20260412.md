# Forsgren / DORA / Accelerate Research

Nicole Forsgren, PhD — co-founded DORA (2015), led the program through Google acquisition (2018), VP Research at GitHub (2020), currently Partner at Microsoft Research.

**Accelerate (2018):** Forsgren, Humble, Kim. IT Revolution. 23,000+ data points. Shingo Award winner 2019. Proved software delivery performance is measurable and predicts organizational outcomes.

**Four Key Metrics:** Deployment Frequency, Lead Time for Changes, Change Failure Rate, MTTR. All measure code-to-production pipeline. Elite: deploy on demand, <1hr lead time, <5% failure rate, <1hr restore.

**DORA Program:** 2014-2025, 39,000+ professionals, annual State of DevOps Reports (now Google Cloud). 2024-2025 added AI research (how AI affects existing metrics — treats AI as a tool, not an agent).

**The gap:** DORA covers code→production. Does NOT cover idea→code (upstream: ideation, research, requirements, design, planning) or production→value (downstream: adoption, feedback, value realization). Does NOT cover agentic development (multi-agent coordination, human-agent workflows, agent quality gates, inter-agent communication).

**Nobody has extended DORA to agentic development.** Confirmed. The gap is real and unaddressed.

**Recommended framing:** "Forsgren Measured Half the Problem. Valueflow measures all of it — and extends it to the agentic world."
