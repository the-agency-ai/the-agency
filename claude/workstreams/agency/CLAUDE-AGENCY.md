# CLAUDE-AGENCY.md — agency workstream

The **agency** workstream is the meta-workstream of the framework. It is where TheAgency is designed, critiqued, extended, and refined — the framework building itself.

This is the only workstream whose subject IS the framework. Every other workstream uses the framework; this workstream evolves it.

## Purpose

Capture the methodology, primitives, and patterns that make TheAgency work. The artifacts here shape how every other workstream operates.

## Scope

- **In scope:** framework methodology (Valueflow, Triangle, Ladder), cross-repo collaboration patterns, telemetry-driven tool discovery, PR workflow conventions, shared philosophy captures (e.g., Gary Tan, Too Lazy to Fail), seed material for articles and books about TheAgency.
- **Out of scope:** implementation details of specific tools (that's `housekeeping` for framework tooling), product workstreams (mdpal, mock-and-mark), and GTM activities (`gtm`).

## Conventions

- **Seeds live at** `claude/workstreams/agency/seeds/` — captured insights that may become references, tools, or framework rules.
- **References live at** `claude/workstreams/agency/references/` — externally-sourced material (articles, talks, other frameworks) with commentary for use in TheAgency's own articles and book.
- **Reviews live at** `claude/workstreams/agency/reviews/` — MAR reviews of framework-level artifacts.
- **Everything committed, everything attributed.** The agency workstream's git history is part of the framework's own learning record.
