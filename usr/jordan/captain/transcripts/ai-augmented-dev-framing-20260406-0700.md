## Key Framing

AI Augmented Development is about **building**, not coding.

In the past, humans coded and built. Coding was the big thing — the primary skill, the bottleneck, the craft.

Now, it is about building. The coding is handled by the agents. The human's role shifts from coder to builder — defining what to build, why it matters, how it should work, and directing the agents who do the coding.

The skill shifts from "can you write this code" to "can you define this problem, design this solution, and direct a team of agents to build it."
